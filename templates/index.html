Our Mission & Vision

To build a safer and disaster resilient India by a holistic, pro-active, technology driven and sustainable development strategy that involves all stakeholders and fosters a culture of prevention, preparedness and mitigation.

  • Disasters Record Management including information of losses of life.
  • Disaster forecasting on the bases of analysis of previous Disasters records
  • Real-time Disaster Losses Graph for easy understanding of disasters scenario.
  • Disasters warning messages distribution by Mobile SMS and by Email

It is not possible to avoid the disasters, but suffering can be minimized by applying effective disaster management. In such situations role of advanced information and communication technology (ICT) is very reliable. Pre disaster management and post disaster management demands rapid access to reliable and accurate data and the capacity to assess, analyse and integrate information from varied sources.

During disasters correct information is as much a necessary as water, food, medicine and support. The right kind of information communicated and used at the right time can save lives, livelihoods and resources. Disaster awareness system and related safety messages are also important to reduce disaster losses.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is our vision?

    To build a safer and disaster resilient India by a holistic, pro-active, technology driven and sustainable development strategy that involves all stakeholders and fosters a culture of prevention, preparedness and mitigation.

  • Why register with us?

    It is not possible to avoid the disasters, but suffering can be minimized by applying effective disaster management. in India, there is no such system who can provide a piece of information and updates through SMS when there is a disaster, information is the key to avoid any panic situation during a disaster and our system provides valid news which will help to overcome the disaster.

  • How to register?

    Follow the following direction given below to register with us:

    1. Visit the website and click on signup option from the navigation bar at right top corner.
    2. Fill in the details and click on signup
    3. That’s it now you are registered with us.

  • What do we do?

    During a disaster if people have right information, they will not get panic and takes the right steps. Our system sends SMS and E-mail during a disaster which will tell them what steps they have to take and guide them how they can be safe and our website will provide the live stats of disaster damage and loss of lives in the disaster, through our system you can also donate money, clothes, blanket, etc. to help the people in need.
